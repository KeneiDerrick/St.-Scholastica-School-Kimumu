About Our School

Our Mission

St. St Scholastica School Kimumu Catholic School provides a Christ-centered education that nurtures the spiritual, intellectual, emotional, and physical development of each student. We are committed to academic excellence within a faith-filled community.

Founded in 1965, our school has served families in the community for over 50 years. We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention and foster a warm, welcoming environment where every student feels valued.

Our dedicated faculty and staff work in partnership with parents to help each child reach their God-given potential while developing strong moral character and a lasting relationship with Jesus Christ.
